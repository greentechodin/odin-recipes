Biryani Recipe

Description:

Biryani is probably the favourite dish of many people in Pakistan. There are many flavors of Biryani available in different areas of Pakistan.

We can have meat based biryani or vegetable biryani.

Ingredients:

Some of the important Ingredients for this recipe include

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Potato
  • Biryani masala

Process:

Making biryani can be easy.

  1. Create curry with potato and chicken, and add Biryani Masala for that particular flavor.
  2. Boil rice.
  3. Create layered organization of both.
  4. Enjoy!
